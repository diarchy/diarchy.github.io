<div><SPAN class="middlee">Diarchy is a two-piece stoner rock band from Bangalore, India. The band was formed in 2015 and they play a variety of rock that's inspired by the sounds of bands like Karma to Burn, Clutch, Colour Haze and Orange Goblin.
